Better yet, new retro game consoles make it possible to revisit our favourite video game worlds, and to get closer to material aspects that have disappeared over time.

In 2021, the first video game from the Geography of Robots collective won a prize at the Tribeca film festival. Norco is an “unreal” game, an audiovisual, pixellated gothic video game science fiction novel. It is also the real hometown of some of the game's creators. Thanks to its retro aesthetic and its minimalist, user-friendly quality, inspired by a 1980s graphic adventure, Norco plunges us into a dystopian Louisiana which seems a little too close to contemporary issues.
